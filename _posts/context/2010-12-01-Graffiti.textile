Around 1980

The other movement spread out in nyc around 1980 is the Graffiti movement. This was the time when young artist struggled to express themselves through their graffiti writing on the subway trains. This subculture was a respond to capitalism usually by young people of lower socioeconomic class at the time in nyc.
