--- layout: default title: Manifesto ---
  1. Always do more than you are expected to do.
  2. Don't be a wannabe. You are special, and you are good at something.
  3. You won't live forever, so do what you think is right.
  4. Be a good time manager. All-nighter is evil and unnecessary.
  5. You choose what you do because it makes you happy.
  6. Nobody hates you. Maybe you are just not good enough... yet.
