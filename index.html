Imaginary Interface: The Poetic Intervention in Web Space
Our culture has been rapidly evolving following the web evolution, while the digital society has been dominated by web corporations, people as the end users are constantly adapting in passive mode. To reverse the systemized thinking, Imaginary Interface goes beyond usability guidelines by creating narratives in the webpage through user interactions. Re-imagining Interfaces helps to provoke individual thinking and imagination in web interactions.
