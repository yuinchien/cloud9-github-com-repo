The Poetic Intervention in Web Space
Our culture has been rapidly evolving following the web evolution, while the digital society has been dominated by web corporations, people as the end users are constantly adapting in passive mode. To break out the pre-defined web systems, The Poetic Intervention sees a website with an url address as public space, and pushes the boundary of interface guidelines by creating extensive narratives on webpages to raise inherent questions or provoke imaginations.
